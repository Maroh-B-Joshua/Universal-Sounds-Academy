Students Elocution Class Training

This training is targeted at helping pupils/students in key stages; basic and secondary classes improve their spoken English and get a better grade in their national English language. Nigeria pupils possess good voice quality but need to polish their pronunciation, rhythm, intonation and clarity to become accurate and sensitive when communicating in English. We believe strongly that these will boost your pupils/students’ performance and make them stand out among their peers in other schools.
